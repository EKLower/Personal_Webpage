Who am I?

I am an interactive and graphic design student at Millersville University. While I enjoy design as much as the next person, it's not my true passion. I've always said my dream job is to be a full time student for the rest of my life. I love learning and I want to learn as much as I can about everything that interests me. This is why I focus more in art history, curation, and archival studies. I want to be able to create things that allow people to explore their passions, just like I explore my passion for learning.
